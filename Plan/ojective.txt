# Online Purchasing System (e-Kiosk)

## Project Overview

The Online Purchasing System is a web-based e-commerce application that enables customers to browse, search, and purchase products from multiple categories through an easy-to-use online platform. The system also provides an administrative interface for managing products, monitoring sales, generating reports, and analyzing product performance.

The application is designed to support both customers and administrators with separate login functionalities and role-based access control.

---

## Key Features

### Customer Features

1. Separate registration and login functionality for customers.
2. Browse and search products category-wise and product-wise.
3. View detailed product information including:

   * Product ID
   * Product Name
   * Description
   * Price
   * Quantity on Hand (QOH)
4. Add products to the shopping cart.
5. Remove products from the shopping cart.
6. Place orders for products added to the cart.
7. Cancel or delete orders.
8. Submit customer feedback and reviews.

### Product Categories

* Electronics
* Electrical
* Furniture
* Cosmetics
* Toys
* Books

---

## Administrator Features

1. Secure administrator login.
2. Add, update, and delete products.
3. Manage product details including:

   * Product ID
   * Product Name
   * Description
   * Price
   * Quantity on Hand (QOH)
4. Monitor sales data on:

   * Weekly basis
   * Monthly basis
   * Quarterly basis
   * Yearly basis
5. Generate reports for:

   * Product sales
   * Customer feedback
6. Analyze fast-moving and slow-moving products based on sales performance.
